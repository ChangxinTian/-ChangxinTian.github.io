Changxin Tian   |   田长鑫

Changxin Tian is a third year graduate student from School of Information, Renmin University of China, under the supervision of Wayne Xin Zhao. He obtained the Bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

His research interests are in Recommender System and Graph Neural Networks. You can contact him by email at tianchangxin AT ruc.edu.cn.
